“Sploosh!”

I hit the water with a splash and sank fast. I closed my eyes and relaxed my mind. The pressure of the water was far from oppressive; in fact, it was quite the opposite: It supported me, made me weightless, for even the heaviest burden is manageable without gravity pulling one down.

And yet, I was sinking.
