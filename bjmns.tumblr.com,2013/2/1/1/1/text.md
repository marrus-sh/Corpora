“Sploosh!”

I hit the water and sank fast. I tried to relax: There was no point in fighting. But the water was cold, and my muscles remained tense. I felt myself starting to go numb as I sank deeper. I exhaled slowly, and tried to clear my mind…

My muscles finally began to loosen, and I felt, for a moment, at peace. Was I still sinking? I couldn't tell. I looked up towards the surface, its light little more than a distant shimmer now. I closed my eyes. Finally, it was over. I had done what I had needed to do.

The water filled my lungs, and I was content.
