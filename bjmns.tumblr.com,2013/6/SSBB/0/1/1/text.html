Super Smash Bros. Brawl


Once there was a guy named Snake. He was one of three clones. His full name was Solid Snake. Snake was awesome. And top-secret. And deadly. One time he decided to hide in his box when he was put on a plane, because they thought it was full of food. The driver was Donkey Kong. Diddy was the co-pilot. When Diddy got hungry, he went to get some food from the storage. He approached Snake's box, but saw it said "Oranges".

“Yummy-dummy! Foooooood!”

As Diddy approached the box, smoke poured out. It was a smoke grenade. Diddy panicked so he shot peanuts at it. One got in the box and hit Snake in the head.

“Owwww!” The box said. It exploded in an attack of gunshot and fists. Diddy flew throught he window and fell… out the plane. Snake almost flew out, but he grabbed onto the door on the other side. As Diddy was falling, he remembered he had a jetpack. He started to fly back up, but Snake threw Donkey Kong out the window. They collided, and crashed to the ground. Snake landed the plane because it had low fuel. He was walking around the airport at night, when he was attacked by… nothing. Yet. He heard rumbling, and as he watched, Metaknight landed his ship on his plane, crushing it. Snake was not happy. Then Snake shot Metaknight's plane, with a rocket launcher. Snake shot off his head. ROB picked up his head and threw it at Snake. It missed and hit Metaknight's ship again. The ship completely blew up.

Now ROB was dead. And Metaknight. Sorry. But then Link met Snake. He seemed friendly, so they teamed up. They soon met Ganondorf and Dark Link. They didn't seem so friendly. So they did a team battle, and Snake got the Final Smash. He shot grenades from his helicopter at Ganondorf and Dark Link. Dark Link got his head blown off. Ganondorf dodged the grenades. He stabbed his sword through Link. This made Link mad, so he punched Ganondorf in the face, 3% damage. Then Link died. Ganondorf laughed and ran away. Snake was kicked off the helicopter for not being able to kill Ganondorf, so he had to walk. He came across Kirby & Sonic playing frisbee. The frisbee went to far and missed Kirby, and crashed into Snake's helicopter, blowing it up. “Oops…” Sonic said. The burning helicopter fell on Kirby, but he used ↓B. But Snake thought he was dead. He attacked Sonic, but Sonic became… Metal Sonic! Snake ran away to save his skin. After all, he wasn't stupid. Sadly, Snake was stupid enough to not know Sonic had super speed! He caught up to Snake and… Kicked him to Skyworld. Snake didn't land on a solid cloud, so he fell through about 6 before Pit could stop laughing and save him. A clawshot flew through the air and latched onto Snake's foot. An arrow shot through the clouds and hit Pit. He fell to the ground. It was ghost Link! Only not a ghost. He was respawned! They found him attacking Zero Suit Samus. The amount of people fighting Ganondorf scared him, so he… Disappeared in a puff of smoke and reappeared with Help.
