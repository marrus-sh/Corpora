Edgar Mantle is twelve years old, has black hair, and brown eyes.  He is somewhat athletic, gets A's and B's in school, is fairly popular, and likes to read.  In other words, Edgar Mantle is just an average kid.  Not an average human kid, though; not exactly.  His dad was human all right, but his mom... that's a different matter.  His mom was a fairy, almost a pure pixie, but with a bit of elf in her, too.  His mom was forty-three, and the two of them lived together on a small, one-story house on a never busy street.  Edgar's dad was dead, and probably had been for hundreds of thousands of years, human time; or roughly twelve years, fairy time.  All fairies, or half-fairies, live in a bubble a few hundred feet underr the earth's surface.  Because of a magical mix-up, time is also slowed to a rate of one fairy day equaling 717 human years.
