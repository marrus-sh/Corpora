There are three realms. Two were natural and nearly identical. The third was man-made, but has the same properties as the other two. In this section I will describe these realms and give their legends...THE REALM OF LIGHT


The People:

The people of this realm are light-skinned, but with a tan. They are couragous and brave, ready to stand up against any attack.


The Features:

This realm has a bright sun and lots ofplants and animals.


The Legend:

The people of this realm are peaseful, and have no great legends.|THE REALM OF DARKNESS


The People:

The people of this realm are very pale and many have irises that are some shade of red. They constantly seek power, and are the strongest people of any realm.


The Features:

The sun of this realm is hot but dim, and the land has little life.


The Legend:

Okay, so life was good, everyone was happy, and, despite it's name, the realm of darkness wasn't all that dark. Its four heros were all good people, and they led the realm toward happiness and freedom. These heros grew old, died, and were replaced with other equally good people. And time passed.

Eventually there came a new hero named Boris. He spoke to the people of his realm about a better life, paridise, in the realm of light. He built an army and destroyed those in his way, even the other heroes. Villages and towns were destroyed, many castles lay in ruin, and as time passed, people forgot why they were doing what they were doing, forgot of the paridise of which Boris spoke, but followed him into battle anyway, not out of hope, but out of fear.THE MIDDLE REALM


The People:

The people of this realm have pale skin, usually with a hint of red, green, or blue. All of these people are very wise, but they are also affected by one of the three forces, usually indicated by the color of their skin (see: Forces). The Heroes of this Realm look different, having dark maroon hair rather than the shades of grey normal to the realm. Heroes here also have skin similar in color to the light realm's, and often have powerful blue eyes. This region has powerful magic, and its heroes are the strongest in this area.


The Features:

This Realm has no sun; light comes from the sky itself. This Light grows brighter in the day and fades at night. The ground is made mainly of a grey rock, but in places there is no rock and one can see the dark grey soil.


The Legend:

There once was a mighty king/hero who desired peace between the realms of light and dark. he proposed the creation of a middle realm, a realm for those unhappy in the land they were in. He gathered the heroes and they created the realm, as well as two Middle mirrors, one light and one dark, for transport between the realms. Those who disliked their realm left, and life on the new realm begun.the mirrors-


The mirrors have been around since the creation of their realm. The following pages describe these mirrors.THE TRUE MIRROR


The true mirror transports one between the realms of light and dark. It is usually kept in the castle, but can be moved to any location. It is silver.THE MIDDLE MIRRORS


These mirrors were created with the middle realm (see: The Middle Realm). They transport you to and from the light/dark realms. The light one is light yellow, the dark is dark violet. In the light/dark realms they are located in Crossroad, in the middle they are in the castle, side by side.the heroes-
